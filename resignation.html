Common questions

Notice period mein resign karna zaroori hai? +

Haan, aapke employment contract ke hisaab se notice period serve karna legally required hai. Agar nahi karte toh company FnF hold kar sakti hai ya recovery claim kar sakti hai. Buyout option ke liye HR se pehle baat karo.

Toxic workplace mein resignation letter kaise likhein? +

Firm mode use karo — clear, professional, bina emotion ke. Resignation letter mein kabhi bhi specific complaints mat likho. Agar legal action ka plan hai toh alag se document karo.

Relieving letter nahi de rahe toh kya karein? +

Pehle written email bhejo (Hinglish Nudge mode se generate karo). Agar 30 din tak nahi milta toh Labour Commissioner ke paas complain kar sakte hain. Relieving letter legally dena company ki obligation hai.

Resignation email better hai ya printed letter? +

Dono bhejo — email se trail rehti hai aur printed letter formal record ke liye. Email subject line mein "Resignation — [Your Name] — [Last Working Day]" likho.
