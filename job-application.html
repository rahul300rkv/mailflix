Pro tips

Job application email tips

01

Subject line matters most

HR gets hundreds of emails. A clear subject like "Application for Software Developer — Rahul Sharma" gets opened. MailFix adds this automatically.

02

Keep it under 200 words

Recruiters don't read long emails. Short and clear wins every time. Mention the role, qualification, and one key skill.

03

Always attach your resume

The email opens the door — your resume closes it. Name it properly: "Rahul_Sharma_Resume.pdf" not "resume_final_v3.pdf".

04

Follow up after 5 days

If no reply in 5 days, send a polite follow-up. Most freshers skip this — it immediately sets you apart.
